Blastfungus Extra Ideas:

Spark Fungus:
Creates little spark particles on breeding, zaps things with lightning if they get too close.
Destroying them chains to nearby spark fungus, killing them too, but harming anything near them.
Blue, glowy.

Rainbow Fungus:
Colour cycle constantly.

Balloon Fungus:
Size increases throughout lifetime (per breed cycle). Do not die normally, breed slowly.
If attached to physics prop, their size directly influences upwards force.
